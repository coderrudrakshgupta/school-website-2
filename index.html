Hi!

Delhi Public School, Faridabad is a premier educational institution of Delhi NCR and one of the core schools of the Delhi Public School Society, which has a rich legacy of having imparted education for more than seventy years and has more than 200 schools under its banner across the globe. DPS Faridabad has 4499 students in its tutelage and 500 teaching and non-teaching staff. The students have shown consistent excellence in academics, sports and cultural activities. Owing to the high standard of quality education being imparted to the students here, the School has been ranked number 1 for the last six years as per the Time Schools Survey. The School provides a congenial and holistic environment to its students. Spread in an area of 8 acres, the School is equipped with the most sophisticated infrastructure to facilitate the academic, sports and co-curricular activities. The well kempt lawns have earned several trophies in the past. The students have earned accolades and coveted International and National awards in various disciplines. Among them there are children who have qualified the world renowned and prestigious Cambridge University Entrance for Arts and Humanities Assessment; will be representing India at Netherlands in EGMO; have represented India in the Youth Asian Shooting Championship at Doha and earned a Gold medal and represented India in the Eurasian Athletic Meet, winning 2 Gold medals. The School boasts 57 international admissions in top 50 world ranked universities this session and remarkable performance of the children in the Board and various competitive exams. At the same time, the School is known for its robust mega productions- Raja Nahar Singh, Subhash Chandra Bose, Krishnanubhuti, Aladdin, Matilda, Kalyanam, - which have not only showcased the latent talent of the children in performing arts but also delivered social messages. Understanding the responsibility of creating thinking and socially responsible individuals, the school has provided platforms like ' PEHCHAAN' and 'SEWAM' wherein the children can contribute to the society and environment through various activities. Thus is very much in sync with the School Motto- 'Service Before Self'. To further the pursuit of inculcating the values of national pride, discipline, hard work and sincerity in students, the School successfully initiated the NCC programme in 2018. The cadets have been diligently carrying their duty during all the major programmes and functions of the School. A proud moment for the School was when two of its cadets got selected for the Republic Day camp. The School believes in respecting the roots and at the same time keeping abreast with the latest technological advancements in school education, thus striking a unique balance between the traditional and the modern. Following the traditional 'Guru ShishyaParampara' on the one hand, and keeping abreast with technological advancement in education, the School has introduced the use of tablets in Classes IX and X, thus minimizing the load of school bags. In this age of digitalization, the use of virtual technology is put to optimum use. All the major correspondence, transactions and communication are executed online to save the use of paper. The Facebook page of DPS Faridabad gives the latest updates about the happenings in the school. The Student Portal was started for students in 2015. Since then, the portal has catered very well to the needs of the students. Class work and home work is available for the students on the portal. It has become convenient for the parents too to raise their query through it. All information and circulars are updated for the students on their portal.The installation of Solar Panels has further helped minimize the carbon footprint. Various Government projects and schemes for the uplift of certain sections of the society have been diligently followed by the School administration. The children of the EWS category are given their due during the admission process. Programmes are organized under "SarvshikshaAbhiyan' and 'BetiBachao, BetiPadhao'. Vocational subjects under PMKVY( PradhanMantriKoushalVikasYojana) have also been started. The School holds all individuals to be equal and doesn't believe in any form of discrimination. As a result, an inclusive environment is propagated in the school to facilitate learning for the differently abled. Provisions have been made in the curriculum as well as in the infrastructure to enable them bea part of the mainstream in a dignified manner. The School is also concerned about the educational and emotional uplift of the staff and students. Various Teacher Training Programmes are organized from time to time to augment the literary and academic acumen of the teaching faculty. The teachers are also made to attend workshops to help them cope with the emotional and psychological challenges. Life skills and mental health workshops are also organized for the students by experts, so that they have a healthy emotional quotient. They are also given career guidance by professionals, so that it is easier for them to decide streams conducive for future careers. The students are taken on educational trips to give them a healthy exposure of their own country and other countries. Student Exchange Programmes enrich the students with international exposure. The highly distinguished alumni have made a distinct mark in the society and still remain connected with its alma mater.
